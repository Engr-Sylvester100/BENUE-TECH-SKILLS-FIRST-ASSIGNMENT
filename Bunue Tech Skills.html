Benue Tech Skills

Benue Tech Skills is a transformative, community-driven initiative committed to delivering accessible, high-quality technology education to individuals from diverse backgrounds, with a particular emphasis on empowering the youth of Benue State. Under the visionary leadership of our digital Governor, His Excellency Rev. Fr. Hyacinth Iormen Alia PhD, this initiative aspires to eradicate digital illiteracy across the state.

Governor Alia's dedication to digital advancement is reflected in this initiative, which aims to equip the youth with essential tech skills that are vital for thriving in the modern world. By providing training in areas such as Software development (Backend and Frontend development), data analysis, Cyber security, and digital marketing among others, Benue Tech Skills not only enhances employment prospects but also fosters innovation and entrepreneurship within the community. Ultimately, the initiative is more than just an educational program; it is a beacon of opportunity, driving the digital transformation of Benue State and setting a benchmark for similar initiatives nationwide. By bridging the digital divide, we are preparing a new generation of tech-savvy individuals who will lead Benue State into a prosperous and technologically advanced future.

Under the guidance of lead consultant Engr. Jeffrey Udende, Benue Tech Skills offers a wide range of courses, such as Cyber security, Backend development, and frontend development among others. These programs are designed to be hands-on and project-based, ensuring that students gain practical experience while learning.

To accommodate different learning preferences and schedules, Benue Tech Skills provides both physical classes and online sessions. This blended approach helps students build professional networks, learn from industry experts, and stay updated with the latest technological trends.
